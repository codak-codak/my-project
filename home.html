What Is Your Purpose?

Author: Okafor Ifunanya

Published: Jan 15, 2021

Mark Twaine once said:

There are two most important dates in your life; first is, the day you're bron, second is the day you figure out why"


God has never created anything without a purpose. God does not create things without a reason. So, if your heart is beating, then you have a purpose. If you want to know the pupose of your life; start with God.
You can't tell you what your purpose is because you didnt create you
The manufacturer/inventor knows the reason for his invention. Talk to your Creator and read the owner's manual.
Ephesians 1:11-12:
It is in Christ that we find out who we are and what we are living for... part of the overall purpose He is working out in everything and everyone
